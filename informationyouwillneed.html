In order to complete the application, you will need to provide copies of the following items:

  • Copies of Income Tax Returns for the last two years

  • Copies of the last four (4) paycheck stubs or other proof of income

  • Copies of the last three (3) bank statements, including name of bank, mailing address and account number(s)

  • Copies of proof of child support and/or public assistance (SSI, Families First, Social Security, retirement benefits, food stamps, etc.), if you receive them

  • Verification of service in the Armed Forces, if applicable.

  • Name, mailing address and telephone number of your current employer

  • Name, mailing address and telephone number of your past employer

  • Name, mailing address of your current landlord

  • Name, mailing address of your past landlord

  • Name, mailing address, and account number of three (3) credit references (auto loan, department store, bank loan, etc.).
