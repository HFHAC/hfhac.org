We have built over 55 houses in Anderson County, TN. With the help of our ministry, more than 220 children and adults now have decent housing.

Founded in 1992, Habitat for Humanity of Anderson County is a Christian ministry that works with diverse individuals, faiths and organizations to build affordable houses for families in need.
