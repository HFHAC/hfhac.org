Sponsoring a house with Habitat for Humanity of Anderson County is an investment that has the power to build a stronger community. Sponsorship not only helps a family in need of decent and affordable housing, but it also makes a positive impact within the community by creating homeowners.

Numerous studies have shown that homeowners are more likely to take an active role in their neighborhoods, participate more fully in churches and civic groups, and have children who are more likely to graduate from high school or college.

Together, we can build more than houses. We can change lives and provide a path to a brighter future filled with hope and prosperity.
