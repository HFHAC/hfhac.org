RecycleBank Rewards Members can now support Habitat for Humanity of Anderson County through RecycleBank. RecycleBank is a rewards program that dramatically increases household recycling rates across the country by rewarding families and individuals with RecycleBank Points for the amount they recycle every day. For those of you who live in a community participating in the RecycleBank rewards program, you can now use your points to support our work! RecycleBank members can redeem their points for coupons at the Habitat Home Store or for real dollar donations to Habitat for Humanity of Anderson County! If you are a member of this program, we encourage you to use your Points to help building simple, decent housing.

Redeeming Points for Cash Donations

1. Go to the RecycleBank website.
2. Login to your RecycleBank account.
3. Click Get Rewards.
4. Click the Donations category.
5. Click the Social Services category.
6. Select Habitat for Humanity of Anderson County from the list. You can redeem 1025 points for a $10 donation.

Redeeming Points for Habitat Home Store Coupons

1. Go to the RecycleBank website.
2. Login to your RecycleBank account.
3. Click Get Rewards
4. Click the Home category.
5. Select Habitat Home store from the list.

If you would like to find out more about RecycleBank, or need instructions on how to activate and use your Points for donations to Habitat for Humanity of Anderson County, go to www.RecycleBank.com or call 888-727-2978.
