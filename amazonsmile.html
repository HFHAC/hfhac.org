Amazon.com, the world's largest e-retailer, has a wonderful service for non-profits called AmazonSmile.

Click on the graphic at the left, register or sign-in, and .5% of all your eligible Amazon purchases will be donated to Habitat for Humanity of Anderson County.

This is an easy way to make a difference for our partner families while you make your holiday purchases.
