The Habitat ReStore is a resale store that accepts donations of new and gently used home furnishings, building supplies, and other household items. Those items are then sold at 10-50% of their retail prices.

Profits from The Habitat ReStore are used by Humanity of Anderson County to fund building projects, creating housing for low-income families in need.

Items accepted

Appliances

  • Items 7 years old or less, in good working order with no repairs needed.
  • Ranges (freestanding ONLY)
  • Ovens (freestanding)
  • Range hoods
  • Washers/dryers
  • Refrigerators
  • Microwave ovens

Architectural Items

  • Columns
  • Mantels
  • Antique windows
  • Porch posts

Cabinets/Countertops

  • Individual pieces or full cabinet sets (kitchen or bathroom). Cabinets must have all doors and drawers and must be in good condition. Countertops must be in good condition with minimal chips or damage. Countertops must be like new.

Doors

  • Pre-hung or door slabs no taller than 84”, free of rot and cracks, all panes intact.
  • Exterior/interior
  • Entryway units
  • Sliding glass - must have all tracking, no cracked or fogged glass

Electrical

  • Wire, plugs, plates, and hardware associated with electrical wiring. Residential items preferred. Needs to be sorted by type or part.

Electronics

  • Working equipment only.
  • Television sets (must be less than 5 years old)
  • DVD and VHS players and/or recorders
  • Audio-receivers, speakers, CD players
  • Stereo equipment

Flooring Materials

  • Carpet - new or “gently-used” and clean with no visible stains or odors
  • Carpet padding - new and clean with no visible stains or odors
  • Hardwood/parquet flooring - free from rot and in usable quantities (25 sq. ft or more)
  • Vinyl tile - undamaged
  • Ceramic tile - undamaged
  • Sheet vinyl/linoleum - any quantity of new material, rolled, taped, and measured

Furniture

  • Only pieces in good condition - no rips, tears, broken parts, or odors. No mattresses or box springs.

Hardware

  • In usable, working condition, free from rust.
  • Complete sets of door hardware, hinges, etc.
  • Any quantity of nails, screws, hangers, connectors, etc.

Insulation

  • New only

Light Fixtures

  • Complete and in good working order

Lumber (framing, trim, pressure treated)

  • Must be free of rot and insects.
  • Plywood - full, ½, 1/4 sheets
  • Milled lumber - 4' lengths or greater
  • Trim - 4' lengths or greater
  • Siding - full lengths

Masonry

  • Whole, intact and in good condition
  • Cinderblock
  • Bricks

Office Materials

  • Office supplies - folders, binders, letter trays, desk organizers, etc.
  • Office furniture
  • Small working office equipment

Paint

  • Sealed, non-rusted cans of latex paint or stain. Color must be identified. No used paint cans.

Plumbing

  • Sinks - all types, no rust, no cracks, no chips, no stains
  • Bathtubs - new or used, good condition, clean, no stains, no chips
  • Shower enclosures - all components
  • Pipe - PVC, metal, copper; 4' lengths or greater
  • Pipe supplies and fittings - any type
  • Faucets(new only)
  • Miscellaneous plumbing hardware

Roofing Materials/Gutters

  • New shingles, full bundles
  • Rolls of roofing material - 1/2 roll minimum
  • Roofing gutter materials in full pieces with no rust or damage

Sheetrock/Wallboard/Drywall

  • 4' lengths and greater.

Shutters

  • Interior and exterior

Storm Doors and Windows

  • Must have all windows and screens and all tracking or framing. No single pane windows.

Tools

  • Hand or power tools in any working condition - will sell as is.

Vents

  • Floor, ceiling, and return. Must be clean and in good condition.

Vinyl Siding

  • Only full, or almost full bundles or packages.

Wallpaper

  • Full, unopened rolls only.

Windows/Screens

  • Double pane minimum - no single pane. Must be in good condition, glass intact. Wooden windows must be free from rot. Must be in complete units with all sashes. All windows must be inspected before acceptance. Metal and wood screens in good condition.

Miscellaneous Items

  • New blinds
  • Books
  • Art work
  • Entertainment items (DVD’s, CD’s, VHS tapes, vinyl records, etc.)
  • Fans - in working condition
  • HVAC and AC units
  • Household items/housewares (dishes, pots, pans, etc.)
  • Mirrors - framed only, no cracks
  • Thermostats
  • Wood stoves - freestanding

Items Accepted for Recycling

  • Inkjet/Toner Cartridges
  • Cell Phones

Items NOT accepted

  • Clothing
  • Mattresses
  • Used blinds
  • Broken/unframed glass or mirrors
  • Bedding, linens, etc.
  • Faucets (used)
  • Garage doors or door openers
  • Paint thinner or other hazardous/toxic chemicals
  • Pesticides (liquid or powder)
  • Used/opened paint
  • Used dishwashers
  • Used trash compactors
  • Water heaters (used)

Please Note: These lists are not exhaustive listings of materials that will or will not be accepted by Habitat ReStore. It will be at the discretion of Habitat ReStore manager to determine what items are accepted for resale and recycling, as well as determine what items are unacceptable.

All items will be priced at 10-50% of their retail value. All sales will be final except with large appliances, which will come with a seven day, money-back guarantee.
